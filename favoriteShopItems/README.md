# FavoriteShopItems  
  
This mod allows you to pin shop items to the top of the shop. It can make it easier to find what you are looking for, as often you have to scroll down through a long list of items in the shop.  

**Instructions:**  
Bring up the context menu (right click on PC) on a shop item you want to favorite and click "ADD FAVORITE". It will go to the top of the shop. Your favorite items will be sorted in order of favoriting them.  
If you want to un-favorite a shop item, just bring up the context menu again and click "REMOVE FAVORITE". It will go back to its previous position.
